Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2014

Information that we may collect during your use of this site may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your academic status
  • Your IP address
  • Courses you are interested or enrolled in
  • Pages of this website that you visit
  • A random anonymous identifier for anonymous behavior logging/analysis
We will use this information to improve your experience while using this site. We will also use your course information in anonymous, aggregate form, to improve the service for you and other users.

We will never sell any of your information to third parties unless it is both anonymous and aggregated. Anonymous means that the information can not be used to identify you, nor can it be connected to you. Aggregated means that the information pertains only to groups of people, not individuals. One fictitious example of anonymous aggregated data is "40% of students that take General Chemistry are Chemistry majors."

This site stores some information on your computer:

  • Session cookie (this allows the site to know that your are signed in)
  • Random anonymous identifier cookie for anonymous behavior logging/analysis
  • Google Analytics cookie for collecting anonymous traffic data
  • User data (not including your password) in localStorage

Some links on this site may point to external pages. This Privacy Policy does not apply to external sites and we take no responsibility for the content of those sites.

We may send you email under some circumstances:

  • To verify your email address
  • If you request to reset your password
  • * If there are changes to a course that you have expressed interest in
  • * To inform you of changes to the site
  • Mandatory notices of major changes to the site
Emails sent under circumstances marked with a * are optional. Each such email will include instructions for unsubscribing from future emails.

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time without notice.
